Provision GrowCube

Prepare the setup command here, then use either a phone tool or a computer helper while connected to the GrowCube Wi-Fi. After the cube joins your home Wi-Fi, come back here and let Home Assistant discover it on your LAN.

1. Prepare Wi-Fi Credentials

This page cannot open a raw TCP socket by itself. It prepares the exact payload that a phone tool or local helper script must send to the GrowCube AP at 192.168.1.125:8800.

2. Send The Command On GrowCube Wi-Fi

  1. Connect a phone or computer to the GrowCube Wi-Fi network.
  2. Send the generated command exactly once to 192.168.1.125:8800.
  3. Wait for a response containing either elea32 with a new IP, or an elea31 error.
  4. Reconnect the device back to your normal home Wi-Fi, then return here.
Success response: elea32#...#<ip>#
SSID not found: elea31#1#1#
Password/connect error: elea31#1#6#
